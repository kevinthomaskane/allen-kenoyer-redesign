Over 39 Years & 3,000 Satisfied Customers

Allen Kenoyer Glass has been a beloved fixture in Lawndale, California since the 1980s. What began as a passion for stained glass artistry has grown into a full-service studio — offering custom design, expert repair and restoration, hands-on classes, and a well-stocked retail shop.

Whether you're a first-time student, a seasoned artisan looking for supplies, or a homeowner dreaming of a custom window, our friendly team is here to help. We coordinate with architects, decorators, and contractors to bring your vision to life.
