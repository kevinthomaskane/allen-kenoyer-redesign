### 6a. Name Inconsistency

**Recommendation:** Standardize to **"Allen Kenoyer Glass"** everywhere (matches website domain and primary branding). The word "Stained" is a keyword but the brand should be consistent. Use "Allen Kenoyer Glass — Stained Glass Studio" as the full descriptor.

### 6b. Phone Number Inconsistency
| Phone | Where Found |
|---|---|
| **(310) 542-6225** | Current website, primary Yelp, most listings |
| **(310) 379-4211** | Houzz, Hubbiz, 2013 blog post (likely old number) |

**Recommendation:** Ensure **(310) 542-6225** is the only phone listed everywhere. Update Houzz and Hubbiz.

### 6c. Founding Year Inconsistency
| Year | Source |
|---|---|
| **1978** | D&B, Yelp business history, NRBBA ("43-year-old business") |
| **1987** | Current website footer ("Crafting beauty and light since 1987") |

**Likely explanation:** The business was **founded in 1978 in Manhattan Beach**, then **moved to the current Lawndale location around 1987**. The website uses the Lawndale move date.

**Recommendation:** Clarify on the website — "founded 1978, serving Lawndale since 1987" — to capture both dates as meaningful signals.
