# Intermediate Stained Glass Patterns | Allen Kenoyer Glass

## Intermediate Patterns

We proudly present the stained glass patterns of Allen • Kenoyer. Art glass patterns for all levels of art glass expertise, including patterns for mirrors and etching. If you have any questions about any of the patterns found in this collection, please [**contact us HERE**](https://allenkenoyerglass.com/contact/). These patterns are copyrighted and for sale, and any copying, downloading, or otherwise duplicating is expressly forbidden.

## How To Order Patterns

Make a list of the pattern numbers that you wish to order, then contact us using one of the methods below. Thank you!

**BY PHONE:**
