# Stained Glass Patterns | Allen Kenoyer Glass

## Patterns

Allen • Kenoyer Glass published their first pattern book in 1981. These pages are a catalog of full-sized patterns from those books, divided into categories for easy browsing. Although some may overlap, we have made an attempt to divide them according to their difficulty, but you must be the final judge of your own capabilities. We hope you enjoy your tour of our pattern catalog.

If you have any questions about any of the patterns found in this collection, please [**contact us HERE**](https://allenkenoyerglass.com/contact/).

These patterns are copyrighted and for sale, and any copying, downloading, or otherwise duplicating is expressly forbidden.
